Step 29. Adding the Revenue KPI, sub indicators and KPI Group Table


Here is the list of measures we will be using for the next steps:
Total_Revenue = SUM(Dubai_RET[Revenue]),
DistinctMonths = DISTINCTCOUNT('Calendar'[YearMonthnumber])
AverageRevenuePerMonth = DIVIDE([Total_Revenue], [DistinctMonths]),
Total_RevenueYoY% = 
VAR CurrentYearRevenue = [Total_Revenue]
VAR PreviousYearRevenue = CALCULATE([Total_Revenue] , DATEADD('Calendar'[Date], -1,YEAR))
RETURN
DIVIDE(CurrentYearRevenue - PreviousYearRevenue, PreviousYearRevenue),
  1. -Ensure that you are in the Home tab at the top of the window and click enter Data in the Data Section of the ribbon.

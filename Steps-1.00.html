Problem Statement 1

In Dubai, the financial data for 2023 reveals a significant concern with revenue (Rev YoY%) plummeting by 69.02% compared to the previous year. This decline aligns with a long-term trend of decreasing revenue, as evidenced by a 60.41% year-over-year decrease since 2013. Although there was a temporary surge in 2021 with a 67.32% increase in revenue, this improvement was not sustained. Furthermore, 2020 saw a 30.87% decline in revenue. Despite a positive development in rental income (Rent YoY%) in 2023, it may not be sufficient to counterbalance the overall decline in revenue, necessitating immediate attention and strategic interventions to address this critical financial issue.
